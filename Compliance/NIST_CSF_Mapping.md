# NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) Mapping

## Overview
This section maps the implemented controls in this project to the NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF) functions.

---

## 1. Identify (ID)
- Asset Inventory created to identify organizational assets
- Assets classified based on sensitivity and ownership

---

## 2. Protect (PR)
- Access Control Policy implemented
- Password Policy enforced
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) recommended
- Least privilege principle applied

---

## 3. Detect (DE)
- Monitoring strategies recommended
- Potential threats such as phishing and malware identified
- Risk register used to track and evaluate risks

---

## 4. Respond (RS)
- Incident Response Policy developed
- Defined response steps:
  - Identification
  - Containment
  - Eradication
  - Recovery

---

## 5. Recover (RC)
- Backup and recovery strategies included
- Recommendations for system restoration and business continuity

---

## Summary
This project aligns with the NIST Cybersecurity Framework by implementing controls across all five core functions: Identify, Protect, Detect, Respond, and Recover.
